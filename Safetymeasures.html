SAFETY MEASURES

There are plenty of risks and threats related to the usage of computers and the Internet. To prevent all these risks and threats, you should follow some basic precautions. Here are some tips that you can use to protect yourself.

  • Young children must use the Internet under the supervision of his/her parents.
  • Always clear your web browser history at frequent periods.
  • Lock or log off your computer when you step away.
  • Pay attention to the web camera LED indicators(they’re red on external devices and blue on laptops).
  • Do not share your private information with anyone on the Internet.
  • Check your phone's privacy settings.
  • Do not share your password with anyone except your parents.
  • Protect your online passwords and strengthen them too.
  • Watch out for 'phishing' e-mails.
  • Keep your communication network secure.
  • Do not download any pirated software or games.
  • Always use a full-service internet security  suite.
  • Avoid posting your personal photos and videos on an online platform without the knowledge of your parents.
  • Do not share private information on doubtful  websites.
  • Monitor the posts you get online.
  • Do not respond to any harassing or threatening messages. Instantly block the sender and inform about any such incident.
  • You can also enable two-step verification to be more secure.
  • Stay away from public Wi-Fi.
  • Keep your family and parents updated about your online activities.
